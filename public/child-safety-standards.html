Child Safety Standards

Effective date: February 11, 2026

Dating App has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, trafficking, or any activity that sexually exploits minors.

1. Strict Prohibition

  • Any CSAE-related content or behavior is strictly prohibited.
  • Any account found engaging in CSAE-related conduct is permanently removed.

2. Age Restriction

  • Our service is intended for adults only (18+).
  • Accounts identified as underage are removed.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

  • Users can report abusive or suspicious behavior through in-app reporting features.
  • Reported content and accounts are reviewed, and enforcement action is taken where violations are found.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement and relevant authorities when legally required.

4. Compliance and Escalation

  • We follow applicable child safety laws and platform policies.
  • Where required by law, we report relevant child sexual exploitation content to appropriate authorities.

5. Contact for Child Safety Concerns

If you need to report urgent child safety concerns, contact us at: support@behar-enterprise.com
