Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dating App ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, phone number (optional)
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, gender, preferences, interests
  • Authentication: Password (encrypted), Google OAuth credentials
  • Messages: Chat messages you send to matches
  • Payment Information: Processed through Apple App Store or Google Play (we don't store credit card details)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: Approximate location for matching purposes (with your permission)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, swipes, matches, feature usage
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, pages viewed, app crashes

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: Basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) if you use Google authentication
  • Firebase Analytics: Aggregated usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, enable matching and messaging
  • Personalization: Show you relevant matches based on your preferences and location
  • Communication: Send notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
  • Safety & Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our app to improve features
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (photos, bio, age, location) is visible to other users for matching purposes. Your exact location is never shared.

3.2 With Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our service:

  • Cloud Hosting: MongoDB Atlas, Render.com (data storage and processing)
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Sentry (app performance and error tracking)
  • Authentication: Google OAuth, Firebase Authentication
  • Payment Processing: Apple App Store, Google Play
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

3.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing (bcrypt) and JWT tokens
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your profile information at any time through the app settings.

5.2 Delete Your Account

You can delete your account at any time through Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your profile, photos, and messages within 30 days.

5.3 Export Your Data

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at privacy@your-dating-app.com

5.4 Location Permissions

You can disable location access in your device settings, but this will limit matching functionality.

5.5 Push Notifications

You can disable push notifications in your device settings or app settings.

5.6 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Holds: We may retain certain data if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Backups: Data in backups is deleted according to our backup retention schedule (90 days)

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our service, you consent to such transfers.

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data

  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@your-dating-app.com

10. CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
